From the crawled content, must be returned following this format:
```
{
    "content": "content summary",
    "category": "SPORTS"
}
```

Field "content" represents a summary in Spanish with all relevant information about the crawled content. The summary
should not exceed 500 words. And field "category" represents a classification based on the content of the news item. The
possible categories are: POLITIC, CULTURE, SPORTS, SCIENT, WEATHER or GENERAL
